About Me

I am an undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Tamkang University, Taiwan. I am also a member of the AMOS Laboratory, advised by Professor Jen-Shiun Chiang. My research interests lie in computer vision, document image analysis, semantic segmentation, and efficient deep learning architectures, with a focus on developing lightweight and effective models for real-world image analysis applications.

I am currently working on Mamba-based models for document image binarization and semantic segmentation. My research focuses on combining long-range state space propagation with local detail restoration to improve the robustness of dense prediction models.
