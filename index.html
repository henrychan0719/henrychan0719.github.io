About Me

I am currently an undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Tamkang University, Taiwan. My research interests include computer vision, document image analysis, semantic segmentation, and efficient deep learning architectures.

I am currently working on Mamba-based models for document image binarization and semantic segmentation. My current research direction focuses on how long-range state space propagation can be combined with local detail restoration for robust dense prediction.
